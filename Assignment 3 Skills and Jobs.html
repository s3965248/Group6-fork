Mobile Application Designer:

Responsibilities:

  • Follow the wireframe created to design the app as well as identifying and planning for new mobile app features where appropriate.
  • Develop APIs (application program interfaces) that support functionality.
  • Work closely and collaborate with the rest of the team.
  • Supply strategic thinking and leadership.

Skills/Experience:

  • Project management – as an app designer you will handle the communication and cooperation between the project team and the client.
  • UI and UX experience – important for end-user satisfaction.
  • Superior design sense – as the app designer you need to be capable of making the app look visually appealing and provide a suitable user experience.
  • Programming language skills – you must be able to use coding languages such as HTML, CSS, Java, etc. effectively.
  • Experience working in a team – must be able to quickly adapt to rapidly changing environments.
  • Effective communication skills.

Requirement/Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Interaction Design, or related field.
  • Excellent design skills with a thorough understanding of typography, colours, intuitive design, and palate development.
  • Thorough understanding of user-experience design for web and mobile.
  • Strong ability to recognise and predict user interaction.
  • Proven ability to deliver high quality designs to customers.

Mobile Developer:

Responsibilities:

  • Discussing the requirements and the proposed solution with colleagues.
  • Support the entire application lifecycle all the way from the concept to the release.
  • Produce fully functional mobile application writing clean code.
  • Troubleshoot and debug to optimise performance.
  • Collaborate with rest of the team to ensure all aspects are functioning correctly.

Skills/Experience:

  • Programming languages such as C#, Java, etc.
  • Strong organisational skills.
  • Ability to interpret and follow technical plans.
  • Problem-solving skills.
  • Effective communication skills.

Requirement/Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology or similar field.
  • Proven work experience as a mobile developer.
  • Ability to perform in a team environment.
  • In-depth knowledge of at least one relevant programming language.
  • Portfolio of released applications.

Quality assurance specialist:

Responsibilities:

  • Manage, perform, and organise user testing for application deployments.
  • Perform quality assurance efforts on multiple application releases.
  • Monitor and document testing activities.
  • Collaborate with team to understand product changes and QA testing requirements to deal with issues promptly.

Skills/Experience:

  • Excellent computer competence.
  • Communication skills, both verbal and written.
  • Data collection and analysis skills.
  • Strong attention to detail.

Requirement/Qualifications:

  • Relevant training and/or certifications as a Quality Assurance Specialist.
  • Proven work experience as a Quality Assurance Specialist or similar role.
  • Knowledge of quality assurance terminology, methods, and tools.
  • Demonstrated knowledge of testing best practices, version control practices, and defect management practices.

Sales and marketing specialist:

Responsibilities:

  • Develop marketing strategies, including using company websites and social media.
  • Undertake competitor research and analyses.
  • Work closely with the team on program development and implementation.
  • Analyse data to determine campaign efficiency.
  • Develop and communicate marketing plans, campaign results and project recommendations.

Skills/Experience:

  • Ability to coordinate and manage design of marketing materials.
  • Strong organisation and prioritisation skills.
  • Proficiency with web analysis tools.
  • Ability to work well in a fast-paced, collaborative environment.

Requirement/Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in marketing, advertising, or related field.
  • Experience in a marketing and or sales role.
